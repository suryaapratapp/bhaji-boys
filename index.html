Our Story

From Mumbai to the Market Square.

Bhaji Boys started in a small kitchen in Cambridge with one goal: to prove that Indian snacks don't have to be heavy or greasy to be delicious.

Whether you're a student needing a study break, or a local on your lunch hour, we're serving up warmth, one cup of chai at a time.
